Use Case 3

Customer Spending Forecast

Predictive Analytics · Multi-Variable Regression

01 · Problem

📋 Problem Description

Modern e-commerce platforms struggle to identify which customers will be high-spenders in the next fiscal year. Without accurate forecasting, marketing budgets are wasted on low-engagement segments. This system utilizes historical behavioral data to calculate the Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), allowing for surgical precision in retention strategies.

03 · Technical Insight
📐 How the CLV Model Works

The model doesn't just look at total spending — it weighs the Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) factors together:

CLV = β₀ + β₁(Recency) + β₂(Frequency) + β₃(Monetary)
🐋
Whale Customers

⚠️
Churn-Risk Customers

💡 By adjusting these weights, the system identifies high-value customers and churn risks before they stop buying.
